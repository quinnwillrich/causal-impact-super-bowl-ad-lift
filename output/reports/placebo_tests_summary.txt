PLACEBO TESTS: Multiple fake intervention dates
================================================

Purpose: assess whether the model produces consistent ~zero effects
on dates when no known intervention occurred. Variance across placebos
tells us about model bias vs. detection of unknown real events.

All placebo dates are Sundays inside the pre-period.
All use a 23-day post-period (matching real analysis).
